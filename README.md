# A Villager's Dream
[![Build Status](https://github.com/Priestytheplushie/AVillagersDream/actions/workflows/build.yml/badge.svg)](https://github.com/Priestytheplushie/AVillagersDream/actions)
![Minecraft Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/Minecraft-1.21.1-brightgreen?style=flat-square)
![Loader](https://img.shields.io/badge/Loader-NeoForge-orange?style=flat-square)

A Villager's Dream is a Minecraft Neoforge 1.21.1 mod which adds brand new Emerald and Villager themed content. 

> [!WARNING] 
> **Early Access**: This mod is still in development, If you find any bugs, report them in Issues

## ✨ Features

* **Emerald Gear:** Brand new set of **Emerald Tools** and **Emerald Armor** with unique Abilities (see below)

<details>
  <summary><b>Emerald Gear</b></summary>
  <br>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Emerald Sword:</b> Deals 6 damage. Deals double damage to Illagers. Grants Strength II during Raids.</li>
    <li><b>Emerald Pickaxe:</b> Has a 20% chance to grant Emerald Rush when breaking blocks, granting Haste and a chance to find Emeralds while breaking blocks.</li>
    <li><b>Emerald Axe:</b> Stripping logs has a 10% chance to turn into Emerald Ore. The chance triples while in a Village and is 100% with Hero of the Village. Stripping logs costs a high amount of durability.</li>
    <li><b>Emerald Hoe:</b>
      <ul>
        <li><b>Right-click:</b> Instantly harvests all fully grown crops in a 3x3 area</li>
        <li><b>Shift-right-click:</b> Till a 3x3 chunk of dirt into farmland</li>
        <li><b>Passive:</b> Using either Ability has a chance to drop Emerald Nuggets.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li><b>Emerald Shovel:</b> Digging a path has a 10% chance to turn into a Emerald Ore.</li>
    <li><b>Emerald Armor:</b></li>
    <ul>
      <li><b>Full Set Bonus: Gilded Greed:</b> Grants an Emerald Barrier based on emeralds held (in inventory), maxing at 10 hearts, but converts shield damage into Debt which must be paid every 15 seconds or the armor suffers extreme damage.</li>
  </ul>
  <hr>
  <b>How to Craft Emerald Gear:</b>
  <p>Emerald gear uses the Smithing System, similar to Netherite:</p>
  <ol>
    <li><b>Smelt</b> an Emerald into an <b>Emerald Ingot</b>.</li>
    <li><b>Acquire</b> an <b>Emerald Upgrade Template</b> (Traded by the Emeraldsmith at their Emerald Workbench)</li>
    <li><b>Combine</b> Iron Gear + Emerald Ingot + Template in a <b>Smithing Table</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  <hr>
  <b>Emerald Workbench:</b>
  <p>The Emerald Workbench is the jobsite block for the Emeraldsmith and allows you to enhance exsisting gear with Qualities.</p>
  <ol>
    <li><b>Craft</b>: 2 Emerald Blocks and 4 Planks into a <b>Emerald Workbench</b>.</li>
    <li><b>Invest</b>: Invest 1-64 Emeralds onto a piece of gear to strengthen the quality</li>
    <li><b>Apply</b> Apply your Quality to your chosen gear to enhance its attributes.</li>
  </ol>
</details>

* **Rubies:** The original, scrapped Villager currency, back with new, fiery powers.

<details>
  <summary><b>Rubies</b></summary>
  <br>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Ruby:</b> A rare drop from Disguised Illagers. Striking a mob with a Ruby sets them on fire.</li>
    <li><b>Ruby Furnace:</b> A dual-input high speed furnace which can smelt 2 items at a time 3x faster than a normal furnace.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

* **Friends and Foes:** Brand new **Scouts** and **Disguised Illagers** to improve Village and Raid encounters. New Villager Profession "Emeraldsmith" to upgrade your iron gear further

<details>
  <summary><b>New Friends and Foes</b></summary>
  <br>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Disguised Illager:</b> Spawns randomly in villages. Looks just like a generic Villager, but get too close and they will reveal their true colors.</li>
    <li><b>Scout:</b> A new Illager joins the ranks! Carrying their trusty horn, this Illager can buff all nearby Raiders. If no Raiders are nearby, they can call in reinforcements.</li>
    <li><b>Emeraldsmith:</b> A new Villager profession who buys Emeralds for various items, including Rubies and Emerald Upgrade Templates</li>
  </ul>
</details>

## 🛠️ Development
This mod is developed using **MCreator**. 

> [!TIP]
> You don't need to build the mod yourself! You can always find the latest "Nightly" version in the **Releases** tab or the latest stable "Ready-to-Play" version on modrinth

* **To Edit:** Download the repository and open the `.mcreator` file in the MCreator IDE.
* **To Build:** Run `./gradlew build` or check the GitHub Actions tab for automated builds.

## 🛠️ Additional Info
* **Modpacks:** You are free to include this mod in any modpack!
* **Requirements:** Built for **NeoForge 1.21.1**.
